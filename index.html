We Believe in the Triple S!

"Savory, Sweet, & Satisfactory!"

At Pat's Salmon Cookie Stand, we focus on making delicous sweet & savory cookies that are not only healthy for you but healthy for salmon too. We are one of the only bakeries in the world that creates almost zero waste! How do we do it? Well, the expired cookies are safe for fish. Yes, you heard right. These cute salmon-shaped cookies are safe for not only human consumption, but for fish consumption as well! All the expired cookies are donated to locate wildlife reservations and fish sanctuires to feed the fish! Stop by Pat's Salmon Cookie Stand today and let's help safe and feed the fish!
