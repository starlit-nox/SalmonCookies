Pat's Salmon Cookies

Welcome to Pat's Salmon Cookies where we focus on not only quality consumption for humans, but for salmon as well! Fresh, daily-made cookies are packed with all the vitiamins and nutritates needed in a healthy balanced diet while handling your sweettooth! Here at Pat's, we focus on as little waste as possible. Because of this, we make sure each Salmon Cookie is safe for the enviroment and for salmon consumption. All expired cookies are fed to salmon to eliminate food waste after expiration!
