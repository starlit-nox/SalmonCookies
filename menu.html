Standard Cookies

    Sugar Salmon

    Sugar Salmon are a fan favorite! This is made from Salmon Pat's secret recipe that uses a healthier sugar subsitute for both fish & friends!

    Choccy Chunk Salmon-Swirls

    Choccy Chunk Salmon-Swirls is one of the most popular standard cookies. It is a sugar cookie with half-melted semi-sweet chocolate chips baked into them to give it a slight choco-swirl!

    Oatmeal Sallie

    Plain Jane? How about Simple Sally! This is just your standard oatmeal cookie.

    Walnut-Salmon

    A dark chocolate cookie with bits of walnuts baked into it.

    Choco Salco

    A simple chooclate cookie.

Premium Cookies

    Salmon Snickerdoodle is a large snickerdoodle cookie with salted carameal drizzled on top! Served warm daily

    *Only avaliable at Seattle & Dubai locations!

    Birthday Salmon

    A large-sized Sugar Salmon cookie that has chocolate or vanilla icing on it with edible glitter & sprinkles! Celebrate your birthday in Sally Salmon Style!

    A half Sugar Salmon and half Choco Salco cookie with white-chocolate chips and semi-sweet milk chooclate-chips.

    *Only avaliable at Tokyo locations!

    The Cosmic Salmon

    Salmon Pat's spin on a galaxy brownie! This is a chocolate cookie brown with pieces of sprinkles baked into it.

    Pecan Pat's

    Pat's signature pecan pie recipe turned into a cookie form! A chocolate-swirl cookie is shaped like a bowl and filled with pecan pie filling. Yum!
